About Me

I’m Anukriti Kaushik, a licensed psychologist dedicated to fostering mental well-being through compassionate and evidence-based practices. With extensive experience in both individual and group therapy, I work closely with individuals to help them navigate life's challenges, including stress, anxiety, depression, and emotional struggles.

Throughout my career, I have supported many clients in understanding and overcoming mental health barriers, empowering them to lead fulfilling, balanced lives. My approach to therapy is client-centered, focused on creating a safe, supportive, and non-judgmental space where individuals can openly explore their thoughts and feelings.

As a specialist in treating anxiety, depression, and stress-related disorders, I use a variety of therapeutic modalities, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Mindfulness-Based Therapy, and Solution-Focused Therapy. I believe in a holistic approach that integrates the mind, body, and spirit, addressing not only psychological symptoms but also lifestyle factors that may contribute to mental health issues. My goal is to provide personalized treatment plans tailored to each individual, ensuring that the therapy process aligns with their unique needs, goals, and personal growth.
